A few of our favorite Mental Health sites

More than ever before, your mental health is more important than ever. With no end in tangible sight, it can feel very isolating and hard to deal with on your own. However, you are VERY much not alone. There are many ways that can help you and others around you be your best selves as we continue to navigate the new normal.

How you can give back during Covid-19

Even if you're quarantined at home, there are still many ways to get involved and give back, including donating money or supplies, volunteering virtually, and checking in on people who might need support. There is always something you can do to help others. And by helping others you will also help yourself.
